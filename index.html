Mortgage Tips

How to Get the Best Mortgage Rate in 2026

Your credit score, down payment, and loan type all impact your rate. A score above 740 typically qualifies for the best available rates. Comparing at least 3 lenders can save you thousands over the life of the loan.

Smart Borrowing

Should You Choose a 15-Year or 30-Year Mortgage?

A 15-year loan builds equity faster and saves on interest, but monthly payments are significantly higher. A 30-year loan offers lower monthly costs with flexibility. Use the calculator above to compare both scenarios side by side.

Interest Rates

Fixed vs. Adjustable Rate Loans Explained

Fixed-rate loans keep the same interest rate for the entire term — predictable and safe. Adjustable-rate loans (ARMs) start lower but can change over time. If you plan to sell or refinance within 5–7 years, an ARM may save money.

The Power of Extra Monthly Payments
