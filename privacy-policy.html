This Privacy Policy explains how LoanIQ ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website and use our free loan and interest rate calculator. By using LoanIQ, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Quick summary: LoanIQ does not collect or store any of the numbers you enter into the calculator. All calculations happen locally in your browser. We do use Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads.

1 Who We Are

LoanIQ is a free financial calculator website providing loan payment estimations, interest rate calculations, and amortization schedules for informational purposes. We are not a bank, lender, financial advisor, or credit institution. Our tools are provided solely for educational and planning purposes.

Our website is operated independently and is not affiliated with any financial institution.

2 Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Information you do NOT provide: Any loan amounts, interest rates, or terms you enter into our calculator are processed entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Automatically collected data: Like most websites, we automatically receive standard server log information when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies: We and our advertising partners (including Google) use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate the website and deliver relevant advertisements. See Section 4 for full details.

We do not require account registration, and we do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable financial information.

3 How We Use Information

The automatically collected data and cookies are used to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the LoanIQ website
  • Analyze traffic patterns and understand how users interact with our tools
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes.

4 Google AdSense & Cookies

LoanIQ uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to LoanIQ and other sites across the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at google.com/settings/ads.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
  • Google's Privacy Policy can be reviewed at policies.google.com/privacy.
LoanIQ uses both first-party cookies (for site functionality) and third-party cookies (for advertising via Google AdSense). You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though this may affect site functionality.

5 Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense – Advertising platform. Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics – Website traffic analysis. Data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Google Fonts – Font delivery via Google servers. No personal data is retained by Google for this purpose beyond standard server logs.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

6 Data Retention

Server log data (IP addresses, browser info, page visits) is retained for a maximum of 90 days for security and analytics purposes, after which it is automatically deleted or anonymized.

As noted above, no calculator input data is ever stored on our servers. All loan calculations are ephemeral and exist only within your browser session.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to opt out: Opt out of personalized advertising via Google's Ads Settings.
  • CCPA (California residents): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • GDPR (EU/UK residents): You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also object to processing or request data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 11.

8 Children's Privacy

LoanIQ is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

9 Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of LoanIQ after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
